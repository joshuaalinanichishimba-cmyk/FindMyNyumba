Frequently Asked Questions

Is FindMyNyumba free to use for students?
Yes! Creating an account and browsing properties is completely free for students. There are no hidden fees.
How are landlords verified?
Landlords must provide a valid National ID or Passport and property information during registration. Our team reviews each submission before approval.
What is a Student Host?
A Student Host is a fellow student who has extra room or bedspace to offer. It's a more affordable and community-driven housing option popular near campuses.
What if I encounter a scam?
Use the "Report this listing" button on any property page. Our team will investigate immediately. Never send money before viewing a property in person.
Can I list a property as a student?
Yes! When registering as a student, choose the "Student Host" option. This allows you to list available bedspaces and connect with fellow students needing housing.
