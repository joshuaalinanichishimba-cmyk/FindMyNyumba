Built for Zambian Students

We started FindMyNyumba because finding affordable, safe student housing in Zambia was too hard. We're changing that.

Our Mission

Making Student Housing Accessible

Every Zambian student deserves a safe, clean, and affordable place to call home while they pursue their education. Too many students spend weeks searching for accommodation, fall victim to scams, or end up in unsuitable housing.

FindMyNyumba was built to solve this problem — a trusted, transparent platform that connects verified students with verified landlords, making the process simple, fast, and safe.

We also empower student hosts — fellow students who want to help by offering affordable bedspaces — creating a community-first approach to student housing.
